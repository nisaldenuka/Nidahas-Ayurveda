About

Ayurveda is a traditional system of medicine that originated in India over 5000 years ago. It emphasizes the use of natural remedies and lifestyle modifications to promote wellness and prevent illness. Overall, skills and experience related to Ayurveda require a deep understanding of its principles and practices, as well as hands-on experience in applying these principles to promote wellness and treat various health conditions.

Skills & Experience

Yoga and meditation

Ayurveda emphasizes the importance of physical, mental, and spiritual well-being. Therefore, an Ayurvedic practitioner may have experience teaching and practicing yoga and meditation.

Knowledge of Ayurvedic principles and practices

This includes a thorough understanding of the three doshas (Vata, Pitta, and Kapha) and their role in maintaining health, as well as the use of herbs, oils, and other natural remedies.

Diagnosis and treatment of health conditions

An Ayurvedic practitioner should be able to diagnose and treat various health conditions using Ayurvedic principles and therapies.
